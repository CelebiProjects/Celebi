Examples
========

The following examples demonstrate typical **Celebi** usage patterns. These tutorials are designed to be concrete, reproducible, and easily followed step-by-step within your local environment.

* **Data Processing**: A guide focused on handling, transforming, and cleaning datasets within the Celebi system.
